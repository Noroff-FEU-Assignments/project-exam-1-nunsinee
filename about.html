"More than hundred of longtail boats line on Ao Nang Beach, Krabi"

Krabi province is a famous and popular destination for tourist from over the world. Krabi has counted the name “ The Emerald of Andaman sea” and sea water here is also green like emerald. Tourist who visit here like to travel around small islands by longtail boat to do sea activities. More than hundred longtail boats that line on Ao Nang beach is a fabulous scenery of Krabi, so I use longtail boat as a logo of my blog and green emerald colour on blog designing to remind user about Krabi.
